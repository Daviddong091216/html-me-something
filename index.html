Illinois University of Urbana-Champaign

I had been studying at U of I at 2002 for 3 months.

The University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign (U of I, Illinois, UIllinois, or colloquially the University of Illinois or UIUC) is a public land-grant research university in Illinois in the twin cities of Champaign and Urbana. It is the flagship institution of the University of Illinois system and was founded in 1867. As of October 2018, 30 Nobel laureates, 2 Turing Award winners, and 1 Fields medalist have been affiliated with the university as alumni, faculty members, or researchers.
